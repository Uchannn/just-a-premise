Returns, Cancellations & Refunds — Just A Premise

Last Updated: October 19, 2025

1. Overview

Because Just A Premise offers digital creative products and made-to-order writing services, all sales are generally final once a product has been delivered or downloaded.

2. Cancellations for Custom Work

You may request a cancellation before work begins. If you cancel after work has started, a partial refund may be issued at the creator’s discretion based on the progress made. If the creator cancels the project for any reason, you will receive a full refund.

3. Refund Policy for Digital Products

All digital product sales are final once the item is delivered or downloaded. Refunds are only available if:

  • The file or download is undeliverable, or
  • The creator cancels the product before release.

Chargebacks filed without prior communication may result in a permanent ban from future purchases or commissions.

4. Third-Party Sales

All transactions are processed through external platforms (e.g., Ko-fi). Those platforms’ own refund and dispute policies also apply. If an issue arises, please contact the creator directly first at Dabblerwitch@gmail.com for resolution.
